AI-Integrated Teaching

These workshops explore how language educators can integrate AI into teaching and learning in thoughtful and practical ways. Sessions focus on building AI literacy for teachers and students, designing classroom activities with AI tools, and addressing emerging pedagogical and ethical questions around AI use in higher education.

All sessions can be adapted to your institutional context and delivered online or in person.

  • Humanistic approaches to AI in the language classroom
  • AI tools for speaking practice and oral feedback
  • Generative AI and language assessment
  • Chatbots and conversational AI in language learning
  • AI literacy for teachers and students
  • Designing AI-integrated classroom tasks
  • Ethical questions: academic integrity, authorship, and AI use
  • Practical AI tools for materials design and lesson planning
